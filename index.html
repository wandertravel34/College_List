Reality-check reminders

  • UCSD and UF are true stretches for a 3.65 / 1200 profile. Both must-haves belong in stretch, not target. Setting expectations early matters.
  • CU Boulder is a legitimate target — right in the fairway.
  • "Biz: Diff" schools have a second admissions hurdle beyond the university admit. Plan on maintaining a strong college GPA freshman year to clear it.
  • WUE is the biggest cost lever for OR/UT/CO/AZ/ID publics, but not all business programs participate — verify per school.
  • Once his SAT score is in: a 1300+ shifts several SEC schools and lower UCs from stretch to target; an 1100 pushes UCs and UF further out and moves a few targets into safety.
  • Verify before applying: acceptance rates fluctuate 3–8 points year over year; net cost after aid can differ dramatically at privates; a few T&F/football program statuses flagged for double-check (LMU, UC Santa Cruz).
